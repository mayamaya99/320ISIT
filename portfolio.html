Align


System Scope:

A tool to align your purchasing power with your moral beliefs. Aligns ensures your money in no way supports organizations or companies that contribute to causes that are antithetical to your beliefs. Align will notify you of products to avoid before adding to your shopping cart when online shopping. An Align user can browse our selection of curated boycotts put together by organizers of social movements, activists or political organizations and create their own boycotts to share with other users.


Project Scope:

The Align application works as a web overlay which is used to provide contextual information on browsed products while online shopping. This first iteration of the project will let a user create a profile, view boycotts, opt in to boycotts and create their own to share


Project Requirement

The goal of the project is to create an application that provides detailed information about boycotts to app users when they make purchases, allowing them to know which products and services to avoid.The application should have the ability to create boycotts, which it does not have.  

Challenges

Our initial challenge is meeting and completing the project due to other team obligations such as family and work.

We were able to overcome the majority of the challenges by working together from the beginning of the project to this point.  Our team's approach to the project is to use HTML, CSS, and Javascript for the front end, and Firebase for the back end.

My experience

The group project was an eye-opening and learning experience for me. The collaboration experience prepared me for a future job in a collaborative environment.It is very easy to pick up new skills during project assignments, just as it is with other team projects; for example, I did not know much about Firebase before, but now I see myself improving. 

The strength that I gained during this group project is the ability to care for my family, work, and complete projects in a timely manner. Everything I went through taught me that if I stay organized and on time, anything is possible.

Advice for those embarking on the same developmental journey  Firstly, you have to be willing to learn something new from anyone and never stop seeking help when you get stuck. Second, you'll be surprised at how much your classmate knows and is eager to share, as well as at how much they value your support.
